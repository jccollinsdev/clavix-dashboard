Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. We don't sell it, and we don't advertise with it.

Last updated: April 12, 2026

What We Collect

Clavix collects only the information needed to deliver your portfolio risk intelligence:

  • Account information — email address for waitlist registration and app access.
  • Portfolio holdings — positions you add manually or connect via linked brokerage accounts.
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app (features used, time spent) to improve the product.

How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Deliver personalized risk analysis and daily briefings.
  • Sync with connected brokerages on your behalf.
  • Send product updates and waitlist communications to your registered email.
  • Improve Clavix features and user experience.

What We Don't Do

Clavix does not:

  • Sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.
  • Use your portfolio data to inform trading decisions for our own account.
  • Store brokerage credentials in plaintext.

Brokerage Connections

When you connect a brokerage account, your credentials are handled via OAuth or read-only API access. Clavix stores only an encrypted access token — never your login password. You can disconnect at any time and revoke access through your brokerage's settings.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Supabase (hosted in the United States) with encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Cookies

Clavix uses minimal session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics scripts.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@getclavix.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we'll post the revised version here with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Clavix after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@getclavix.com.
