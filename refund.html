Refund Policy

Simple, no tricks. Here's how it works.

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Your first month is free. Clavix offers a free trial period for all new users. No credit card required to start. If you're not satisfied, you never pay.

Free Trial

New Clavix users receive a complimentary trial period. During this time, you have full access to all features. You will not be charged unless you explicitly convert to a paid subscription before the trial ends.

Subscription Billing

After your free trial, your selected subscription plan will auto-renew monthly or annually (depending on your plan) until you cancel. Billing occurs on the same date each cycle.

Requesting a Refund

We offer refunds for paid subscriptions under the following conditions:

  • Within 7 days of your first charge — Contact support@getclavix.com and we'll process a full refund, no questions asked.
  • After 7 days — Refunds are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Contact us with your reason and we'll do our best to accommodate.

How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time from within the Clavix app or by emailing support@getclavix.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will not receive a refund for the current period, but you will not be charged again.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days. The refund will be credited to the original payment method used at signup.

Contact

To request a refund or cancel, email support@getclavix.com with your account email and reason. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
